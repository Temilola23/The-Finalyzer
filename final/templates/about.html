Our Purpose

This project is a web-based stock analysis tool designed to help users make informed investment decisions. The tool leverages real-time data from Yahoo Finance to provide comprehensive insights into various stocks. Users can select a stock ticker from popular screeners such as most active, day gainers, or day losers.

Overall, the tool aims to empower users with accurate and timely stock data, making it easier to evaluate and decide on potential investments.
